Certificates

ISO 9001:2015 Certification

Packworld is proud to have achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification from SGS, a renowned international certification body.
This milestone demonstrates our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and continuous improvement,
reinforcing our dedication to delivering exceptional products and services that meet the highest standards of quality and reliability.

"Packworld is proud to have achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification from SGS, demonstrating our commitment to quality,
customer satisfaction, and continuous improvement in delivering exceptional products and services."

FSC COC Certification

This esteemed certification, awarded by the Forest Stewardship Council, recognizes our commitment to responsible
forestry practices, transparency, and traceability in our supply chain.By obtaining FSC CoC certification, Packworld reaffirms
its dedication to environmental stewardship and delivering ecofriendly products that meet
the highest standards of sustainability and social responsibility.

"FSC CoC certification recognizes Packworld's commitment to responsible forestry, transparency, and eco-friendly products,
reaffirming our dedication to sustainability and social responsibility."
