ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE
ABOUT COMPANY

Packworld, based in Karachi, is a leading manufacturer of corrugated packaging solutions, providing a diverse range of products in various dimensions and configurations. With an impressive annual production capacity of approximately 10 million boxes, we are well-equipped to meet the demanding requirements of our clients for high-quality, eco-friendly packaging. Our cutting-edge machinery and advanced printing technology enable us to design and produce multi-color packaging solutions with precision and accuracy.
Our extensive product portfolio includes Regular Slotted Cartons, tray packaging, shelving units, pizza boxes, and customized solutions tailored to meet the specific needs of clients across various industries. At Packworld, we are committed to sustainability and innovation, continuously exploring new materials and methods to enhance the environmental friendliness of our products.
Our dedicated team of experts works closely with clients to understand their unique packaging needs, delivering solutions that protect and preserve their products while effectively promoting their brand. We prioritize quality at every stage of the manufacturing process, ensuring our boxes meet the highest standards of durability and functionality. Additionally, our efficient production processes and strategic supply chain management enable us to offer competitive pricing and timely delivery, making us a reliable partner for businesses of all sizes. Whether it's a small batch order or a large-scale production run, Packworld is equipped to handle it with the utmost professionalism and care.

Why Choose Us

Choosing Packworld as your corrugated carton manufacturer offers several compelling advantages:

Quality Products: Packworld is known for producing high-quality corrugated cartons that meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our products are designed to be durable, reliable, and efficient for various packaging requirements.

FSC CoC Certification: We are proud to offer FSC Chain of Custody certified cartons, ensuring that our products are sourced from responsibly managed forests. This certification reflects our commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility.

Customization: At Packworld, we understand that each client has unique packaging needs. We offer customized solutions to fit your specific requirements, whether it's size, design, or functionality.

Innovation and Technology: We continuously invest in the latest technology and innovative processes to ensure our products are at the forefront of the industry. This allows us to provide high-performance packaging solutions that are efficient and cost-effective.

Customer Service: Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional customer service. From the initial consultation to the delivery of your order, we are committed to ensuring your complete satisfaction.

Competitive Pricing: We strive to offer competitive pricing without compromising on quality. Our efficient manufacturing processes enable us to provide cost-effective solutions for our clients.

Reliability: With years of experience in the industry, Packworld has built a reputation for reliability. We consistently deliver on our promises, ensuring your packaging needs are met on time, every time.

Choosing Packworld means partnering with a company that values quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. We are committed to helping your business succeed by providing superior corrugated packaging solutions.
